# Finance Glossary (Synthetic) – FP&A Copilot

**Actuals**: Recorded results for a period.  
**Budget**: Approved financial plan for a period.  
**Forecast**: Updated estimate of expected results.  
**Variance**: Difference between Actual and Budget/Forecast (absolute and %).  
**Favourable**: Better than plan (higher revenue or lower costs).  
**Unfavourable**: Worse than plan (lower revenue or higher costs).  
**Driver**: A factor explaining change (volume, pricing, mix, spend).

## Narrative Style Rules
- State the month and comparison baseline (vs Budget and/or vs Forecast).
- Call out top 2–3 drivers only.
- Do not invent drivers not supported by data or provided notes.
- If data is insufficient, ask a clarifying question (Review Mode).
